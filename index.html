Getaway Flea Market

Date: 04/09/2024 (Eid al-Fitr)-04/11/2024

Time: 10:00 AM-11 PM

Location: Rabat, Morocco

We Have...

Exquisite Handcrafted Goods

Explore a treasure trove of Moroccan craftsmanship. Admire intricate mosaic lamps, colorful ceramics, and ornate brass lanterns. Each piece tells a story of skilled artisans who have passed down their traditions through generations.

Jewelry

Iron Lantern

Carpet

Traditional Textiles

Discover a kaleidoscope of fabrics and textiles, from luxurious silk scarves to traditional djellabas and kaftans. Moroccan textiles are renowned for their intricate patterns and vivid colors, making them a beloved part of the nation's culture.

Berber Scarf

Spices and Cuisines

Your senses will come alive as you stroll past stalls laden with aromatic spices, saffron, cumin, and more. Be sure to savor some delicious street food, like tagine and couscous.

Couscous

Tagine of Chicken

Henna Artistry

Experience the age-old art of henna tattoos. Skilled artists will adorn your hands with intricate and temporary henna designs, a practice deeply ingrained in Moroccan culture.
